We Don't Talk Anymore
Charlie Puth ft. Selena Gomez
"We Don't Talk Anymore" is a song by American singer-songwriter Charlie Puth,
featuring vocals from fellow American singer Selena Gomez. It was released on
August 3, 2016, as the third single from Puth's debut studio album, Nine Track Mind.
The artists wrote the song with Jacob Kasher Hindlin.

Musically, it is a pop song with Puth's tropical-inspired production.
